About ICI Kochi Centre

Indian Concrete Institute (ICI) is a leading professional body in India, catering to professionals, organizations and Institutions in the area of Concrete. ICI is a non-profit organization, dedicated to the cause of knowledge dissemination on concrete and its related areas. Main objective of ICI is to promote Concrete Technology, Construction and related aspects and address the research needs in the area of Concrete. Established in 1982, ICI has since grown into a large organization of repute with Regional Centre’s all over India. Today ICI has more than 14,000 professional members and 45 Regional Centre’s Pan-India. ICI has 363 organizational Members and there are more than 277 Students’ Chapters all over the country. All segments of cement and concrete industries are widely represented in the membership of ICI.

ICI-Kochi has been consistently conducting varieties of programs like Seminars, Workshops, Conferences, Exhibitions, etc. throughout the year attracting active involvement and promoting a large number of professionals in the Construction & Infrastructure Industry. ICI - Kochi has also partnered with GRACE, Structural Engineers Association, ACE & LENSFED for various Events in Kochi.
