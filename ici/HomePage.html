ICI PC Programmes

Indian Concrete Institute (Pune Centre) shares the vision of ICI in propagating and disseminating knowledge beneficial to the construction field. ICI PC organizes a number of programs and events to spread awareness and capability building of the engineering fraternity.

Monthly Tech Talks

Indian Concrete Institute (Pune Centre) brings in experts from the field to deliver captivating monthly webinars and tech talks. Join us to expand your knowledge and stay updated with the latest developments in the construction industry.

Training Sessions

ICI Pune Centre can arrange training sessions tailored to the specific needs of your organization. Get connected with us to enhance the skills and capabilities of your team and stay ahead in the construction field.

Student Chapters

Start a Student Chapter in your college and provide your students with valuable opportunities for growth. Our student chapters offer training sessions, industry interactions, and many more benefits to help students excel in the field of engineering.
